Melty Blood: Actress Again: Current Code

Melty Blood: Actress Again: Current Code (MBAACC) is a fighting game published by video game company, French-Bread. This game's story and characters are based on the cult-classic visual novel created by Type-Moon called Tsukihime. MBAACC is known for its fast-paced gameplay and unique sounding music. The game's music was composed by Raito, who is also known for composing for French-Bread's other long-time standing fighting game series, Under Night In-Birth.

This game is kinda awesome.
